What the source contains

Public records, mapped as points and areas

MassGIS publishes point locations for buildings, burial grounds, structures, and objects; polygons for historic areas and districts; and a town layer indicating survey digitization status. This demonstration intentionally displays only the area/district and town layers. Archaeological-resource information is excluded from the public datalayers.

Use with care

Consult the current record

Locations and attributes reflect source records of varying age and accuracy and may not represent present conditions. Checking this map does not replace required MHC or regulatory review.
