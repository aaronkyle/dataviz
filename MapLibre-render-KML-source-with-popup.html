Standalone MapLibre template

Render KML with MapLibre.

Fetch a KML file, convert it to GeoJSON, and draw it on a token-free map. Hover to compare regions; select one to inspect its attributes.

  • No access token
  • KML → GeoJSON
  • Responsive extent

Three steps

  1. Fetch the KML as text.
  2. Convert it with toGeoJSON.
  3. Add layers and fit to the data.

Reuse it by changing KML_SOURCE_URL and MAP_COLORS. View the source KML.
