Why these areas matter

Massachusetts designates Areas of Critical Environmental Concern for the quality, uniqueness, and significance of their natural and cultural resources. Designation supports stewardship and stricter review of some projects under state jurisdiction.

A demonstration, not a regulatory map

MassGIS states that these mapped boundaries are for planning purposes. Written designation documents—not this display—provide the definitive boundary description, and field review or ACEC program assistance may be needed.
